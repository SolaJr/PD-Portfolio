‍
Tools used: Star CCM, Solidworks, Matlab, Excel, User Research
Project Overview
 A prover is an automated system that provides on-site calibration to ensure flow meters in service for fiscal and custody transfer applications maintain sustainable measurement performance as well as remain in compliance with industry standards.
Need Statement
The Daniel Compact Prover has not undergone significant changes in many years and is in need of an update to remain competitive in the market. The team will review the current compact prover and suggest ways to improve the current design to launch the next generation compact prover. Costs and ease of maintenance will be key factors to consider for the team.
-Analyzed dynamic behaviors of Emerson’s Daniel Compact Prover variants 
-Investigated dynamic fluid behavior for alternative design concepts to determine feasibilities of implementation using STAR-CCM+ andSolidWorks 
-Developed SolidWorks models to represent conceptual design models and animations 
-Proposed approaches to optimize performance of proposed designs
