Balloon Grid

Roles - Product Manager, Researcher, Data Engineer, Prototype Designer
Tools - Solidworks, Woodworking tools, Multi-variable calculus, UX Research, Metalworking tools
Project Overview
Modern architecture has been moving away from concrete buildings due to high expenses associated with complex designs. However, since most of the cost for a building comes from the labors developing it, we decided to create a press that could simplify the process of making concrete roofs. So we developed a multi-point actuator to simplify the process of making presses for the concrete. That way, instead of wasting thousands of dollars on wood and materials to build frames, our Balloon Grid press can form them with a single person.
